NON-PROFIT · EDUCATION · TECHNOLOGY

Technology education for every child, everywhere.

BrightRoot brings AI, robotics, and semiconductor education to children and youth in rural and underserved communities — with or without internet access.

WHAT WE TEACH

Three pillars of future-ready learning & real-world application

Basic AI & Machine Learning

Introduction to how AI thinks and learns through games and physical activities.

Robotics & Automation

Build and program robots to learn logic, sequencing, and engineering skills.

Semiconductors & Electronics

Understand the chips and circuits that power our modern world.

Beyond the Classroom

We don't just teach theory. Our programs focus on Science Projects and Real-life Problem Solving, empowering students to build Startups and solutions that address challenges in their own villages.
